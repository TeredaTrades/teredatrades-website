Core Framework

Trade what you see, not what you hope.

Most traders fail because they react to candles. We teach you to read the story behind price — where institutions move liquidity, how structure shifts, and when probability stacks in your favor.

Liquidity

Price doesn't move randomly — it seeks liquidity. Learn to identify buy-side and sell-side pools, recognize structural shifts, and position yourself where smart money is already headed.

  • Equal highs & lows as magnets
  • Inducement before the real move
  • Targeting resting orders

Market Structure

Structure is your roadmap. Master break of structure (BOS), change of character (CHoCH), and premium vs. discount zones to know exactly where you are in the cycle.

  • Higher highs & higher lows
  • Order blocks & fair value gaps
  • Multi-timeframe alignment

Execution & Risk

A perfect setup means nothing without discipline. We build your edge with defined entries, position sizing, and a psychology framework that survives drawdowns.

  • Risk-to-reward minimums
  • Session-based trading windows
  • Journal & review systems
