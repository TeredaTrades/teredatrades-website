Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tereda Trades ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit teredatrades.com (the "Site") or submit an application through our mentorship enrollment form.

Information We Collect

When you submit the application form on this Site, we collect:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Your stated trading experience level
  • Your program of interest
  • Any details you provide about your trading roadblocks or goals

We do not knowingly collect payment information, government ID numbers, or other sensitive personal data through this form.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit to:

  • Evaluate and respond to your mentorship application
  • Contact you about our programs via email or Telegram
  • Maintain internal records of applicants and communications

Third-Party Services

When you submit the application form, your information is sent to the following third-party services for the purposes described:

  • HubSpot — used as our customer relationship management (CRM) system to store and manage applicant records.
  • Google Sheets / Google Apps Script — used as a backup record of submissions to ensure no application data is lost.

These providers process data on our behalf and may store information on servers located outside your country of residence. We encourage you to review HubSpot's and Google's own privacy policies for details on how they handle data.

Cookies

This Site may use a HubSpot tracking cookie to associate form submissions with your visit and to help us understand engagement with the Site. This cookie does not collect sensitive personal information.

Data Retention

We retain application data for as long as reasonably necessary to evaluate your application, respond to your inquiry, and maintain business records, unless you request deletion sooner.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To make such a request, contact us through our Telegram channel.

Children's Privacy

This Site and our mentorship programs are not directed at, and are not intended for use by, anyone under the age of 18, or the age of majority in their jurisdiction, whichever is greater. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to us via Telegram, Discord, or YouTube.
